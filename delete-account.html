Delete Your Account

Tripp

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Tripp account and the data associated with it. You can delete your entire account, or just specific items, at any time — directly from within the app.

Delete your whole account

  1. Open Tripp and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account and associated data are deleted immediately.

Delete specific data (without deleting your account)

You can remove individual items at any time while keeping your account:

  • Photos — open a magnet, select the photos, and delete them.
  • Magnets — open a magnet and choose Delete to remove it and its photos.
  • Journal entries — delete any country journal entry from the journal screen.
  • Trips — leave a trip, unlink a magnet, or delete photos you added to a trip board.

Deleted items are removed immediately.

If you can’t access the app

Email tripp.application@gmail.com from the email address on your account, with the subject “Delete my account”. We will process your request within 30 days.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your profile (name, username, and home city)
  • All of your magnets and the photos you uploaded
  • Your badges and country journal entries
  • Trip boards you created, and the photos you added to trips

What is kept

No personal data is retained after deletion. Residual copies in encrypted backups are removed in line with our standard backup retention schedule.

Contact us

If you have questions about deleting your account, contact us at tripp.application@gmail.com.
