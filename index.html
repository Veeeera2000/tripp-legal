Privacy Policy

Tripp

Last updated: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tripp (“the app,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information. By using Tripp you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign up we collect your name and email address. You can sign in with Google (via Google OAuth) or with your email address using a one-time code.
  • Location. With your permission, we use your device’s location once, during profile setup, to pre-fill your home city. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Photos and images. Photos you capture or upload (for example, when scanning a magnet) are stored in your account and used to provide the app’s features.
  • App content. Information you create in the app, such as trips, collections, and badges you earn.

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you.
  • To provide core features: scanning magnets, building collections, creating trip boards, and awarding badges.
  • To process images you submit, including identifying content within a photo.
  • To improve, secure, and maintain the app.

Third-party services

We rely on the following service providers to operate Tripp. Your data is shared with them only as needed to deliver these features:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Google — Google Sign-In (OAuth) for authentication.
  • Google Gemini — when you scan a photo, the image is sent to Google’s Gemini API to identify its contents. Images are sent for processing and are not used by us to train models.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy.

Data retention

We keep your account information and content for as long as your account is active. Photos that are no longer linked to your account are periodically removed by an automated cleanup process.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account from within the app. Deleting your account removes your profile and associated content from our systems. If you need help, contact us at the address below.

Children’s privacy

Tripp is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at vpmgoncalves@gmail.com.
